Start up project

My start-up idea is called Let's Talk Tacos. Provo is full of people looking for fun dates. They want food, but not too expensive. They want a sit down restaurant while still having a casual, fun atmosphere to talk and have fun. Tacos check these boxes. They are simple, versatile, and loved by many. But how do you know which taco restaurant to go to? There are so many options. Let's Talk Tacos is here to help.

This idea sparked from a simple date idea: try one taco from five different places and rank them. This website picks a random taco place within a specified mile radius to go and try. The website also displays additional information, such as address, photos, and menu. You can then add the place to your list, and generate another to try as well. After eating at the restuarant, the website prompts you to rank it based on taste, atmosphere, and overall satisfaction. An easy, effective date idea for taco enthusiasts, friends, and couples!
